Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer known for his incredible athleticism and goal-scoring ability. He has won five Ballon d'Or awards and achieved success in England, Spain, and Italy.

Pelé

Pelé was a Brazilian footballer who is the only player to win three FIFA World Cups. He scored over 1,000 goals and is considered one of the greatest footballers in history.

Diego Maradona was an Argentine footballer famous for his dribbling skills and the "Hand of God" goal. He led Argentina to World Cup victory in 1986 and is considered one of the sport's all-time greats.

Johan Cruyff
