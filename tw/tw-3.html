Lionel Messi

Argentine forward widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time. Record eight Ballon d'Or winner and 2022 World Cup champion.

Cristiano Ronaldo

Portuguese superstar known for incredible athleticism and goal-scoring. Five-time Ballon d'Or winner with success across multiple leagues.

Pelé

Brazilian legend and only player to win three World Cups. Scored over 1,000 career goals and global football ambassador.

Diego Maradona

Argentine magician famous for dribbling skills and 1986 World Cup heroics. "Hand of God" and "Goal of the Century" creator.

Johan Cruyff

Dutch master who revolutionized football with "Total Football." Three-time Ballon d'Or winner and influential manager.

Zinedine Zidane

French maestro known for elegance and technical brilliance. 1998 World Cup winner and successful Real Madrid manager.

Ronaldo Nazário

Brazilian phenomenon known as "Il Fenomeno." Two-time World Cup winner and one of the most complete strikers in history.

Alfredo Di Stéfano

Argentine-Spanish legend who dominated European football with Real Madrid. Won five consecutive European Cups.
