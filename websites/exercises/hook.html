Welcome to Hook Head Lighthouse

Discover Ireland's Ancient Coastal Beacon

Guided Tours

Join our knowledgeable guides for a fascinating tour through one of the oldest operational lighthouses in the world. Learn about the lighthouse's history, architecture, and maritime significance.

The guided tours provide insights into the daily life of lighthouse keepers and the technology used to keep sailors safe throughout the centuries.

Panoramic Views

Climb to the top of Hook Head Lighthouse for breathtaking views of the rugged coastline and the surrounding Atlantic Ocean. Perfect for photographers and nature enthusiasts.

On clear days, you can see distant cliffs and islands, making it an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages.

Visitor Centre & Exhibits

Explore our interactive visitor centre showcasing the history of Hook Head and its maritime heritage. Exhibits include historic maps, shipwreck stories, and lighthouse equipment.

The centre also offers educational activities for children and a gift shop featuring local crafts and souvenirs to remember your visit.
